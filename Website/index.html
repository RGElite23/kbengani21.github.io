Intro

Currently, the method for creating personalized playlists on Spotify is inefficient and inconvenient. The process involves tediously searching for different songs and manually adding each song to a playlist. However, with the use of our Robotic Process Automation, music enthusiasts can create a personalized playlist with just a click of a button!

About

Using UiPath, the automation will create a personalized playlist based on the user's favorite artists. The program will ask for a name in order to create the playlist. From there, UiPath will automatically start to data scrape the user's "liked songs" and extract the most common artists and convey it on an Excel spreadsheet. Then, the automation searches up every artist and retrieves their three most popular songs (based on relevance) and adds them to the user's playlist. This process is repeated for every artist on the spreadsheet. At the end of the automation, users will have a personalized playlist full of songs that they are sure to enjoy! What could have been a lengthy process is easily completed within a short span of a few minutes!
